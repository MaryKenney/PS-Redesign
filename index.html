Founded in 1980, Pocket Sandwich is the last of the Dallas dinner theatres (although the food and beverage service is optional). We are very proud of the theatre’s large and faithful patron base and of the fact that almost half of our shows are productions of comedies by local playwrights. Come see why we're "the most fun you can have in a Dallas theater!"
