Ticket Prices

Our ticket prices vary depending on the show and the night of the week. All bands and comedians set their own ticket prices and you can check out all shows and pricing on our "Coming Up" tab.


Our Plays Typically Cost:

  • Thursday nights- $12.50
  • Friday nights- $21.50
  • Saturday nights- $27.50
  • Sunday nights- $16.50

Exceptions

These prices are subject to change. Musicals are priced a little higher because of increased production costs. We will also have exceptions on holidays, for example, Christmas and Valentine's Day. There is a $2 discount on Thursdays through Sundays for Seniors (60+) and Children (12 or under).


If you have any additional questions, please contact us down below or using our box office at 214-821-1860.

Reservations

Please Note:

All online sales are final. Orders must be purchased together or under the exact same name in order to be guaranteed sitting together. All seats are pre-set by reserved party sizes.

*special needs (ada) seating must be made by calling our Box office at 214-821-1860.

Please arrive by at least 15 minutes before showtime to claim your seats, earlier for food and drink. Dinner service ends 30 minutes prior to show time. Unclaimed seating is opened to the public 5 minutes prior to show time.
